# 640 — 家政科學 Home Economics

> 家政科學是研究家庭生活與資源管理的應用科學——涵蓋食品營養、服裝紡織、居家管理、兒童發展、家庭關係與消費者教育。
> Home Economics is the applied science of family life and resource management — from food and nutrition to child development and consumer education.

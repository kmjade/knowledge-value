# 黄帝内经-灵枢-邪客第七十一

## 📖 原文

黄帝问于岐伯曰：邪气之中人也，奈何？

岐伯曰：邪气之中人，有浅有深。浅者客于皮毛，深者客于筋骨。客于皮毛者，则为寒热，客于筋骨者，则为痛不仁。邪气之中人，亦有轻重。轻者伤于卫，重者伤于荣。伤于卫者，则病在表；伤于荣者，则病在里。

黄帝曰：邪气何以客于人？

岐伯曰：邪气之客于人，有五：一曰风雨，二曰寒暑，三曰饮食，四曰居处，五曰忧思。风雨伤人，则客于皮毛；寒暑伤人，则客于经络；饮食伤人，则客于肠胃；居处伤人，则客于肌肉；忧思伤人，则客于脏腑。

黄帝曰：善。然则邪客于皮毛者，何如？

岐伯曰：邪客于皮毛，则腠理开，腠理开则邪气入，邪气入则卫气不固，卫气不固则寒热作。寒热作，则汗出，汗出则津液伤，津液伤则形体损。此邪客于皮毛之变也。

黄帝曰：善。然则邪客于经络者，何如？

岐伯曰：邪客于经络，则血气凝泣，血气凝泣则不通，不通则痛，痛则经脉拘急，经脉拘急则肢体不用。肢体不用，则痿痹作。痿痹作，则神机不用，神机不用则形体不立。此邪客于经络之变也。

黄帝曰：善。然则邪客于肠胃者，何如？

岐伯曰：邪客于肠胃，则肠胃不和，肠胃不和则传导失司，传导失司则升降失常，升降失常则清浊不分，清浊不分则呕吐泄利。呕吐泄利，则津液耗，津液耗则形体虚。形体虚，则邪气深入。邪气深入，则病难愈。此邪客于肠胃之变也。

黄帝曰：善。然则邪客于脏腑者，何如？

岐伯曰：邪客于脏腑，则脏腑不调，脏腑不调则气血乖乱，气血乖乱则阴阳失和，阴阳失和则生病焉。生病焉，则形体不安。形体不安，则精神不定。精神不定，则百病丛生。百病丛生，则天年不终。此邪客于脏腑之变也。

## 🌐 白话文翻译

黄帝问岐伯说：邪气侵犯人体，是怎样的呢？

岐伯说：邪气侵犯人体，有浅有深。浅的停留在皮毛，深入的停留在筋骨。停留在皮毛的，则出现寒热；停留在筋骨的，则出现疼痛麻木。邪气侵犯人体，也有轻重。轻的损伤卫气，重的损伤营气。损伤卫气的，则病在表；损伤营气的，则病在里。

黄帝说：邪气为什么会侵犯人体呢？

岐伯说：邪气侵犯人体，有五种情况：一是风雨，二是寒暑，三是饮食，四是居处，五是忧思。风雨伤人，则停留在皮毛；寒暑伤人，则停留在经络；饮食伤人，则停留在肠胃；居处伤人，则停留在肌肉；忧思伤人，则停留在脏腑。

黄帝说：好。那么邪气停留在皮毛，是怎样的呢？

岐伯说：邪气停留在皮毛，则腠理张开，腠理张开则邪气进入，邪气进入则卫气不固，卫气不固则寒热发作。寒热发作，则汗出，汗出则津液受伤，津液受伤则形体受损。这是邪气停留在皮毛的变化。

黄帝说：好。那么邪气停留在经络，是怎样的呢？

岐伯说：邪气停留在经络，则血气凝滞，血气凝滞则不通，不通则痛，痛则经脉拘急，经脉拘急则肢体不能活动。肢体不能活动，则痿痹发作。痿痹发作，则神机不用，神机不用则形体不能站立。这是邪气停留在经络的变化。

黄帝说：好。那么邪气停留在肠胃，是怎样的呢？

岐伯说：邪气停留在肠胃，则肠胃不和，肠胃不和则传导失职，传导失职则升降失常，升降失常则清浊不分，清浊不分则呕吐泄利。呕吐泄利，则津液消耗，津液消耗则形体虚弱。形体虚弱，则邪气深入。邪气深入，则疾病难愈。这是邪气停留在肠胃的变化。

黄帝说：好。那么邪气停留在脏腑，是怎样的呢？

岐伯说：邪气停留在脏腑，则脏腑不调，脏腑不调则气血紊乱，气血紊乱则阴阳失和，阴阳失和则生病。生病则形体不安。形体不安则精神不定。精神不定则百病丛生。百病丛生则天年不能终尽。这是邪气停留在脏腑的变化。

## 🌍 English Interpretation

The Yellow Emperor asked Qi Bo: When pathogenic qi invades humans, what is the situation?

Qi Bo replied: When pathogenic qi invades humans, it can be superficial or deep. The superficial stays in the skin and hair; the deep stays in the sinews and bones. When it stays in the skin and hair, it causes cold-heat; when it stays in the sinews and bones, it causes pain and numbness. Pathogenic qi invading humans also has severity - the mild damages wei qi, the severe damages ying qi. When wei qi is damaged, the disease is in the exterior; when ying qi is damaged, the disease is in the interior.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. Then why does pathogenic qi invade humans?

Qi Bo replied: Pathogenic qi invading humans has five situations: first, wind and rain; second, cold and heat; third, food and drink; fourth, dwelling place; fifth, worry and thought. Wind and rain harm people, staying in the skin and hair; cold and heat harm people, staying in the meridians; food and drink harm people, staying in the stomach and intestines; dwelling place harms people, staying in the muscles; worry and thought harm people, staying in the zang-fu organs.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. Then when pathogenic qi stays in the skin and hair, what is the situation?

Qi Bo replied: When pathogenic qi stays in the skin and hair, the couli opens. When the couli opens, pathogenic qi enters. When pathogenic qi enters, wei qi cannot consolidate. When wei qi cannot consolidate, cold-heat manifests. When cold-heat manifests, sweating occurs. When sweating occurs, body fluids are damaged. When body fluids are damaged, the body form is harmed. This is the change when pathogenic qi stays in the skin and hair.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. Then when pathogenic qi stays in the meridians, what is the situation?

Qi Bo replied: When pathogenic qi stays in the meridians, qi and blood congeal and stagnate. When qi and blood congeal and stagnate, they cannot flow through. When they cannot flow through, pain occurs. When pain occurs, meridians become tense. When meridians become tense, the limbs cannot move. When the limbs cannot move, wei-bi (flaccidity and impediment) manifests. When wei-bi manifests, the shen-ji (spirit mechanism) is not used. When the shen-ji is not used, the body form cannot stand. This is the change when pathogenic qi stays in the meridians.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. Then when pathogenic qi stays in the stomach and intestines, what is the situation?

Qi Bo replied: When pathogenic qi stays in the stomach and intestines, the stomach and intestines are disharmonious. When the stomach and intestines are disharmonious, transmission loses control. When transmission loses control, ascending and descending become abnormal. When ascending and descending become abnormal, clear and turbid are not separated. When clear and turbid are not separated, vomiting and diarrhea occur. When vomiting and diarrhea occur, body fluids are consumed. When body fluids are consumed, the body form becomes weak. When the body form is weak, pathogenic qi penetrates deeply. When pathogenic qi penetrates deeply, the disease is difficult to heal. This is the change when pathogenic qi stays in the stomach and intestines.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. Then when pathogenic qi stays in the zang-fu organs, what is the situation?

Qi Bo replied: When pathogenic qi stays in the zang-fu organs, the zang-fu organs are discordant. When the zang-fu organs are discordant, qi and blood become disordered. When qi and blood become disordered, yin and yang lose harmony. When yin and yang lose harmony, illness arises. When illness arises, the body form is not at peace. When the body form is not at peace, the spirit-mind is not stable. When the spirit-mind is not stable, hundreds of diseases arise together. When hundreds of diseases arise together, the natural lifespan cannot be completed. This is the change when pathogenic qi stays in the zang-fu organs.

## 🔍 核心要点

### 邪气侵犯人体的特点
- **浅深有别**：浅者客于皮毛，深者客于筋骨
- **轻重不同**：轻者伤于卫，重者伤于荣
- **表里差异**：伤卫则病在表，伤荣则病在里
- **症状各异**：客皮毛则寒热，客筋骨则痛不仁

### 邪气侵犯的五种途径
- **风雨**：客于皮毛
- **寒暑**：客于经络
- **饮食**：客于肠胃
- **居处**：客于肌肉
- **忧思**：客于脏腑

### 邪客皮毛的病理过程
- **腠理开**→**邪气入**→**卫气不固**→**寒热作**
- **寒热作**→**汗出**→**津液伤**→**形体损**

### 邪客经络的病理过程
- **血气凝泣**→**不通**→**痛**→**经脉拘急**
- **经脉拘急**→**肢体不用**→**痿痹作**→**神机不用**→**形体不立**

### 邪客肠胃的病理过程
- **肠胃不和**→**传导失司**→**升降失常**→**清浊不分**
- **清浊不分**→**呕吐泄利**→**津液耗**→**形体虚**→**邪气深入**→**病难愈**

### 邪客脏腑的病理过程
- **脏腑不调**→**气血乖乱**→**阴阳失和**→**生病焉**
- **生病焉**→**形体不安**→**精神不定**→**百病丛生**→**天年不终**

## 📚 理论解释

### 邪气分层理论
本章提出了邪气侵犯人体由浅入深的五层理论：皮毛→经络→肠胃→肌肉（肌表）→脏腑，体现了中医对疾病发展规律的深入认识。这种由表及里、由浅入深的传变规律是中医辨证论治的重要基础。

### 五脏六气致病理论
风雨、寒暑、饮食、居处、忧思五种致病因素，涵盖了外感六淫、内伤七情、饮食不节、起居失常等多种病因，体现了中医关于病因的全面认识。

### 气血津液生命观
从"邪气入则卫气不固"到"津液伤则形体损"，本章强调了气血津液是人体生命活动的基本物质，外邪侵犯首先损伤人体的防御系统，进而影响气血津液的正常代谢。

### 整体病理观
邪客不同部位产生不同症状和病理变化，但最终都会导致"形体不安"、"精神不定"，体现了中医形神合一、整体观念的思想。疾病的严重程度与邪气停留的部位、正气损伤的程度密切相关。

### 传变规律认识
"邪气深入，则病难愈"揭示了疾病由浅入深、由轻转重的传变规律，对临床预后判断有重要指导意义。早期治疗、防止病邪深入是中医治病的重要原则。

## 🏥 实践应用

### 辨证论治
1. **病位辨证**：根据邪客不同部位确定病位
2. **病性辨证**：区分寒热虚实，明确病理性质
3. **深浅辨证**：判断邪气停留的深浅程度
4. **预后判断**：邪气在皮毛易愈，在脏腑难治

### 针灸治疗
1. **邪客皮毛**：取风池、风门、大椎、合谷等解表
2. **邪客经络**：循经取穴，疏通经络
3. **邪客肠胃**：取中脘、天枢、足三里调理脾胃
4. **邪客脏腑**：取背俞穴调理相应脏腑

### 药物治疗
1. **解表法**：麻黄、桂枝、荆芥、防风等
2. **疏通经络**：当归、川芎、赤芍、桃仁等
3. **调理脾胃**：陈皮、半夏、茯苓、白术等
4. **调理脏腑**：根据脏腑不同选用相应药物

### 预防调摄
1. **避邪法**：避免风寒暑湿外邪侵袭
2. **调饮食**：饮食有节，不过饥过饱
3. **慎起居**：起居有常，不妄作劳
4. **调情志**：保持心情舒畅，避免忧思郁结

### 现代临床意义
1. **感染性疾病**：外邪客表类似于现代感染性疾病的初期阶段
2. **免疫性疾病**：外邪入侵与机体免疫反应的相关性
3. **消化系统疾病**：饮食不节导致的胃肠功能紊乱
4. **精神心理疾病**：忧思伤神与心理疾病的联系
5. **慢性病传变**：疾病由浅入深的传变规律

# 650 — 管理科學 Management Science

> 管理是通過他人完成工作的藝術與科學——涵蓋規劃、組織、領導、控制四大職能，從策略到執行，從人力到品質。
> Management is the art and science of getting things done through others — from strategy to execution, from people to quality.

# Building Science Resources 資源收集

## 📖 Essential Books

| Book | Author | Topic |
|------|--------|-------|
| *Building Construction Illustrated* | Francis D.K. Ching | Construction methods |
| *Structures: Or Why Things Don't Fall Down* | J.E. Gordon | Structural intuition |
| *Heating, Cooling, Lighting* | Norbert Lechner | Sustainable design |
| *A Pattern Language* | Christopher Alexander | Design patterns |
| *The Death and Life of Great American Cities* | Jane Jacobs | Urban planning |

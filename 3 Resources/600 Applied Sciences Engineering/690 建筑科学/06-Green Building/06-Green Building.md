# 06 — Green Building 綠色建築

> Green building minimizes environmental impact while maximizing occupant health, comfort, and productivity.
> 綠色建築在最大化居住者健康、舒適和生產力的同時，最小化環境影響。

## Rating Systems 評估體系

| System | Origin | Focus |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **LEED** | US (USGBC) | Most widely used globally |
| **BREEAM** | UK | Oldest system (1990) |
| **Green Mark** | Singapore | Tropical climate focus |
| **WELL** | International | Health & well-being |
| **Passive House** | Germany | Ultra-low energy |
| **GBL** (綠色建築標章) | Taiwan | Local certification |

## Design Strategies

| Strategy | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Passive Design** | Orientation, shading, natural ventilation |
| **High-Performance Envelope** | Insulation, air sealing, high-performance glazing |
| **Efficient HVAC** | Heat pumps, energy recovery ventilators |
| **Renewable Energy** | Solar PV, geothermal, wind |
| **Water Efficiency** | Low-flow fixtures, rainwater harvesting, greywater |
| **Sustainable Materials** | Recycled content, local sourcing, low-VOC |
| **Daylighting** | Maximize natural light, reduce electric lighting |

## Net-Zero Energy

```
Energy Use − On-site Renewables = 0 (or net-positive)
```

Key to achieving:
1. Reduce demand (passive design)
2. Increase efficiency (equipment, lighting)
3. Generate on-site (solar, geothermal)

## Embodied Carbon vs Operational Carbon

| Type | Definition | % of Lifecycle |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| **Embodied** | Carbon from materials + construction | ~30–50% |
| **Operational** | Carbon from building use (heating, cooling) | ~50–70% |

> 💡 The greenest building is the one already built — retrofit before rebuild. Operational efficiency matters most.

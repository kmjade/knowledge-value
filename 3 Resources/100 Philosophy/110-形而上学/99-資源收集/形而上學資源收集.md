## 三、關鍵論文 Key Papers

### 本體論 Ontology
- Quine, W.V.O. (1948) "On What There Is" — ontological commitment
- Lewis, D. (1986) "Against Structural Universals"
- van Inwagen, P. (1990) *Material Beings* — special composition question
- Schaffer, J. (2010) "Monism: The Priority of the Whole"

### 因果 Causation
- Lewis, D. (1973) "Causation" — counterfactual theory
- Woodward, J. (2003) *Making Things Happen* — interventionism

### 自由意志 Free Will
- Frankfurt, H. (1969) "Alternate Possibilities and Moral Responsibility"
- van Inwagen, P. (1983) *An Essay on Free Will* — consequence argument
- Pereboom, D. (2001) *Living Without Free Will*
- Strawson, P.F. (1962) "Freedom and Resentment"

### 心身問題 Mind-Body
- Nagel, T. (1974) "What Is It Like to Be a Bat?"
- Jackson, F. (1982) "Epiphenomenal Qualia" / (1986) "What Mary Didn't Know"
- Chalmers, D. (1995) "Facing Up to the Problem of Consciousness"
- Putnam, H. (1967) "Psychological Predicates" (multiple realizability)

### 時間 Time
- McTaggart, J.M.E. (1908) "The Unreality of Time"
- Prior, A.N. (1967) *Past, Present and Future*
- Lewis, D. (1976) "The Paradoxes of Time Travel"

### 模態 Modality
- Kripke, S. (1963) "Semantical Considerations on Modal Logic"
- Lewis, D. (1968) "Counterpart Theory and Quantified Modal Logic"
- Fine, K. (1994) "Essence and Modality"

### 當代 Contemporary
- Fine, K. (2001) "The Question of Realism"
- Schaffer, J. (2009) "On What Grounds What"
- Sider, T. (2011) *Writing the Book of the World*
- Thomasson, A. (2014) *Ontology Made Easy*

## 五、閱讀順序建議 Reading Order

### 初學者路徑 (0-6 months)
```
Mumford, Metaphysics: VSI
    ↓
Lowe, A Survey of Metaphysics
    ↓
Conee & Sider, Riddles of Existence
    ↓
Kripke, Naming and Necessity (Lectures 1-2)
    ↓
Nagel, "What Is It Like to Be a Bat?"
    ↓
Frankfurt, "Alternate Possibilities"
```

### 進階研究路徑 (6-18 months)
```
Lewis, On the Plurality of Worlds (selected chapters)
    ↓
Chalmers, The Conscious Mind
    ↓
van Inwagen, An Essay on Free Will
    ↓
Sider, Writing the Book of the World
    ↓
Schaffer, "On What Grounds What"
    ↓
Thomasson, Ontology Made Easy
```

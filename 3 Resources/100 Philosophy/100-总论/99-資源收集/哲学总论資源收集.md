# 哲學總論資源收集（Resources — Philosophy General）

本文件匯集 DDC 100 哲學總論相關的推薦書籍、線上課程、學術期刊與數位資源。

---

## 入門書籍（Introductory Books）

| 書名 | 作者 | 年份 | 說明 |
|------|------|------|------|
| *The Problems of Philosophy* | Bertrand Russell | 1912 | 簡潔的哲學導論，涵蓋知識、實在、真理 |
| *What Does It All Mean?* | Thomas Nagel | 1987 | 極簡入門，適合完全初學者 |
| *Think: A Compelling Introduction to Philosophy* | Simon Blackburn | 1999 | 清晰有趣，涵蓋核心主題 |
| *Sophie's World* | Jostein Gaarder | 1991 | 小說形式的哲學史 |
| *The Story of Philosophy* | Will Durant | 1926 | 以人物為線索的哲學史敘事 |
| *Philosophy: The Basics* | Nigel Warburton | 1992 | 按主題組織的入門書 |
| *The Philosophy Book: Big Ideas Simply Explained* | DK | 2011 | 圖解式哲學概念說明 |

## 經典原著（Classic Primary Texts）

### 古代
- Plato — *Republic*, *Meno*, *Theaetetus*, *Phaedo*
- Aristotle — *Nicomachean Ethics*, *Metaphysics*, *Organon*

### 近代
- Descartes — *Meditations on First Philosophy* (1641)
- Spinoza — *Ethics* (1677)
- Locke — *An Essay Concerning Human Understanding* (1689)
- Hume — *An Enquiry Concerning Human Understanding* (1748)
- Kant — *Critique of Pure Reason* (1781/1787), *Groundwork of the Metaphysics of Morals* (1785)

### 現當代
- Nietzsche — *Beyond Good and Evil* (1886), *Thus Spoke Zarathustra*
- Wittgenstein — *Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus* (1921), *Philosophical Investigations* (1953)
- Sartre — *Being and Nothingness* (1943), *Existentialism is a Humanism* (1946)
- Rawls — *A Theory of Justice* (1971)
- Kuhn — *The Structure of Scientific Revolutions* (1962)

## 推薦閱讀順序（Suggested Reading Sequence for Beginners）

```
1. Nagel, What Does It All Mean?           ← 建立直覺
2. Warburton, Philosophy: The Basics       ← 系統框架
3. Russell, The Problems of Philosophy     ← 經典深度
4. Plato, Meno + Descartes, Meditations    ← 原著入門
5. Blackburn, Think                        ← 鞏固擴展
```

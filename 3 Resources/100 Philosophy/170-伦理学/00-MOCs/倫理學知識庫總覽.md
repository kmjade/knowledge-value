## 關鍵辯論 / Key Debates

| Debate 辯論 | Positions 立場 | Modern Relevance 現代意義 |
|:------------|:---------------|:-------------------------|
| Moral realism vs anti-realism | Can moral claims be objectively true? | Foundation for all normative ethics |
| Consequentialism vs deontology | Do ends justify means? | Trolley problem, autonomous weapons, whistleblowing |
| Reason vs emotion in morality | Hume vs Kant, Greene's dual-process | Criminal justice, AI moral reasoning |
| Universalism vs relativism | Are moral truths culture-bound? | Human rights, cultural imperialism debates |
| Internalism vs externalism | Must moral judgments motivate action? | Moral education, psychopathy, akrasia |

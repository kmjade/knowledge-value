# 04 德性倫理 / Virtue Ethics

Virtue ethics shifts the central question of ethics from "What should I do?" to "What kind
of person should I be?" 德性倫理將倫理學的核心問題從「我該做什麼」轉向「我該成為什麼樣的人」。

## 亞里士多德的幸福論 / Aristotle's Eudaimonia

### Eudaimonia 幸福/興盛
Not mere pleasure (*hedone*) but **flourishing** — living well and doing well as a human being.
Eudaimonia is the final end (*telos*) of human life: that which we seek for its own sake, not
for the sake of anything else.

> "The function of man is an activity of soul in accordance with reason." — *Nicomachean Ethics* I.7

### The Ergon Argument 功能論證
1. A good X is one that performs its characteristic function (*ergon*) excellently
2. The human function is rational activity (*psyches energeia kata logon*)
3. Therefore, the good human life is rational activity performed excellently — i.e., in accordance with virtue

## 中庸之道 / The Doctrine of the Mean

Virtue is a disposition (*hexis*) to choose the intermediate between excess and deficiency,
relative to us, as determined by reason (*phronesis*).

| Sphere of Action | Deficiency 不足 | **Virtue 德性 (Mean)** | Excess 過度 |
|:-----------------|:----------------|:----------------------|:------------|
| Fear & Confidence | Cowardice 怯懦 | **Courage 勇氣** | Rashness 魯莽 |
| Pleasure & Pain | Insensibility 無感 | **Temperance 節制** | Licentiousness 放縱 |
| Money (giving) | Stinginess 吝嗇 | **Generosity 慷慨** | Prodigality 揮霍 |
| Honor | Humility 謙卑過度 | **Magnanimity 大度** | Vanity 虛榮 |
| Anger | Spiritlessness 麻木 | **Patience 耐心** | Irascibility 暴躁 |
| Social Conduct | Cantankerousness | **Friendliness 友善** | Obsequiousness |
| Truthfulness | Self-deprecation | **Truthfulness 真誠** | Boastfulness |

### Phronesis 實踐智慧 / Practical Wisdom
The intellectual virtue (*arete dianoetike*) that enables us to discern the mean in particular
circumstances. Unlike scientific knowledge (*episteme*), phronesis deals with particulars and
cannot be reduced to a decision procedure.

## 安斯康姆的復興 / Anscombe's Revival

G.E.M. Anscombe's "Modern Moral Philosophy" (1958) sparked the modern virtue ethics revival:

**Three Theses 三大論點**:
1. Moral philosophy should be set aside until we have an adequate philosophy of psychology
2. "Obligation" and "duty" are remnants of a law conception of ethics that no longer makes sense without a divine lawgiver
3. Modern moral philosophers (from Sidgwick onward) are indistinguishable — all consequentialist

Anscombe recommended returning to Aristotle and the concept of human flourishing.

## 麥金泰爾的傳統 / MacIntyre's Tradition

Alasdair MacIntyre's *After Virtue* (1981) offers a narrative-based virtue ethics:

**Three Key Concepts 三大概念**:
1. **Practice 實踐**: A cooperative activity with internal goods (e.g., chess, medicine, philosophy)
2. **Narrative Unity 敘事統一**: A human life is a story; virtues enable us to live a unified narrative
3. **Tradition 傳統**: Virtues are sustained and transmitted within living traditions

MacIntyre's famous choice: "Nietzsche or Aristotle?" — reject the Enlightenment project and
choose between Nietzsche's nihilism and Aristotle's teleology.

## 赫斯豪斯的新亞里士多德主義 / Hursthouse's Neo-Aristotelianism

Rosalind Hursthouse (*On Virtue Ethics*, 1999) defends virtue ethics as a complete normative theory:

**Action Guidance 行動指導**:
- "An action is right iff it is what a virtuous agent would characteristically do in the circumstances"
- Virtues benefit their possessor: they enable flourishing

**The V-rules 德性規則**:
Virtue ethics can generate rules: "Be honest," "Be charitable," "Be courageous"
— just as deontology generates "Do not lie."

## 情境主義批判 / The Situationist Critique

| Critic | Claim | Response |
|:-------|:------|:---------|
| **Gilbert Harman** (1999) | There are no character traits; situation explains behavior | Virtue is rare but real; situationist experiments show average subjects, not the virtuous |
| **John Doris** (*Lack of Character*, 2002) | Empirical psychology undermines virtue ethics | Virtues are ideals, not empirical generalizations; local rather than global traits |
| **Milgram Experiment** | 65% obeyed to maximum shock — where is the virtue of compassion? | The experiment shows virtue requires cultivation, not that it's impossible |

## 比較：三種德性倫理 / Three Varieties of Virtue Ethics

| Variety | Basis | Key Thinker |
|:--------|:------|:------------|
| **Eudaimonism 幸福論** | Virtues promote flourishing | Aristotle, Hursthouse, Foot |
| **Agent-Based 主體基礎** | Right action defined by admirable motivation | Michael Slote |
| **Target-Centered 目標中心** | Virtues hit the target of their characteristic aim | Christine Swanton |

# 倫理學資源收集 / Ethics Resource Collection

Curated resources for studying DDC 170-倫理學. 倫理學學習資源精選。

## 核心書籍 / Core Books

### 元倫理學 / Meta-Ethics
| Title | Author | Year | Why Read |
|:------|:-------|:-----|:---------|
| *Ethics: Inventing Right and Wrong* | J.L. Mackie | 1977 | Classic defense of error theory; the argument from queerness |
| *Principia Ethica* | G.E. Moore | 1903 | Naturalistic fallacy; open question argument; intuitionism |
| *Ruling Passions* | Simon Blackburn | 1998 | Quasi-realism and expressivism |
| *Wise Choices, Apt Feelings* | Allan Gibbard | 1990 | Norm-expressivism |
| *The Moral Problem* | Michael Smith | 1994 | Internalism, the Humean theory of motivation |
| *Ethical Intuitionism* | Michael Huemer | 2005 | Contemporary defense of moral realism & intuitionism |

### 規範倫理學 / Normative Ethics
| Title | Author | Year | Why Read |
|:------|:-------|:-----|:---------|
| *Groundwork of the Metaphysics of Morals* | Immanuel Kant | 1785 | The categorical imperative — foundation of deontology |
| *Utilitarianism* | John Stuart Mill | 1861 | Higher/lower pleasures; the harm principle |
| *Nicomachean Ethics* | Aristotle | ~350 BCE | Eudaimonia, doctrine of the mean, phronesis |
| *The Right and the Good* | W.D. Ross | 1930 | Prima facie duties; pluralist deontology |
| *After Virtue* | Alasdair MacIntyre | 1981 | Virtue ethics revival; critique of Enlightenment |
| *On Virtue Ethics* | Rosalind Hursthouse | 1999 | Neo-Aristotelian virtue ethics as complete normative theory |
| *A Theory of Justice* | John Rawls | 1971 | Veil of ignorance; difference principle |
| *What We Owe to Each Other* | T.M. Scanlon | 1998 | Contractualism: reasonable rejection |

### 應用倫理 / Applied Ethics
| Title | Author | Year | Why Read |
|:------|:-------|:-----|:---------|
| *Practical Ethics* | Peter Singer | 1979 | Applied utilitarian analysis of real-world issues |
| *Animal Liberation* | Peter Singer | 1975 | Speciesism; animal ethics |
| *The Trolley Problem* (article) | Judith Jarvis Thomson | 1976 | Now-classic thought experiment |
| *Killer Robots* (article) | Robert Sparrow | 2007 | Autonomous weapons and responsibility gap |
| *A Defense of Abortion* (article) | Judith Jarvis Thomson | 1971 | Violinist analogy; bodily autonomy |
| *The Case for Animal Rights* | Tom Regan | 1983 | Deontological animal rights |

### 道德心理學 / Moral Psychology
| Title | Author | Year | Why Read |
|:------|:-------|:-----|:---------|
| *The Righteous Mind* | Jonathan Haidt | 2012 | Social intuitionism; moral foundations theory |
| *Moral Tribes* | Joshua Greene | 2013 | Dual-process theory; trolley problem neuroscience |
| *Against Empathy* | Paul Bloom | 2016 | Case for rational compassion over empathy |
| *In a Different Voice* | Carol Gilligan | 1982 | Care ethics; critique of Kohlberg |
| *The Emotional Construction of Morals* | Jesse Prinz | 2007 | Sentimentalist meta-ethics |

### 比較倫理 / Comparative Ethics
| Title | Author | Year | Why Read |
|:------|:-------|:-----|:---------|
| *The Analects* (論語) | Confucius | ~500 BCE | Foundation of Confucian ethics |
| *Mencius* (孟子) | Mencius | ~300 BCE | Development of Confucian moral psychology |
| *The Bodhicaryavatara* | Shantideva | ~700 CE | Mahayana Buddhist ethics |
| *Ethics for the New Millennium* | Dalai Lama | 1999 | Contemporary Buddhist ethics |
| *Caring: A Feminine Approach* | Nel Noddings | 1984 | Care ethics foundation |
| *Confucian Role Ethics* | Roger Ames | 2011 | Role-based interpretation of Confucian ethics |

## 案例研究 / Case Studies

### Classic Ethical Dilemmas 經典道德困境

| Case | Description | Theories Engaged |
|:-----|:------------|:-----------------|
| **The Trolley Problem** | Switch Track / Footbridge | All normative theories, moral psychology |
| **The Violinist** | Thomson's abortion analogy | Rights, bodily autonomy, deontology |
| **Jim and the Indians** | Bernard Williams' integrity objection | Utilitarianism, agent-relativity |
| **The Lifeboat** | Who to save with limited resources? | Justice, utilitarianism, care ethics |
| **The Plank of Carneades** | Two shipwreck survivors, one plank | Self-defense, necessity defense |
| **The Heinz Dilemma** | Kohlberg: steal drug to save dying wife? | Moral development, deontology vs care |

### Modern Cases 現代案例
| Case | Ethical Dimensions |
|:-----|:-------------------|
| **Self-Driving Car Dilemmas** | Trolley problem in engineering; moral machine experiment |
| **COVID-19 Triage** | Healthcare allocation; age-based vs lottery vs QALY |

## 建議閱讀路徑 / Suggested Reading Pathway

### Beginner 初學
1. Sandel, *Justice: What's the Right Thing to Do?*
2. Singer, *Practical Ethics*
3. Blackburn, *Being Good: A Short Introduction to Ethics*

### Intermediate 中級
1. Mill, *Utilitarianism*
2. Kant, *Groundwork* (Sections 1–2)
3. Aristotle, *Nicomachean Ethics* (Books I–II)

### Advanced 進階
1. Rawls, *A Theory of Justice*
2. Smith, *The Moral Problem*
3. Scanlon, *What We Owe to Each Other*

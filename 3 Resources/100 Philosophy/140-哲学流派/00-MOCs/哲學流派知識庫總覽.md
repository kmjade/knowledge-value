## 二、流派比較矩陣 School Comparison Matrix

| 流派 | 形而上學 Metaphysics | 認識論 Epistemology | 倫理學 Ethics | 方法 Method |
|------|---------------------|---------------------|--------------|-------------|
| **古希臘** | Substance, Forms, atoms | Reason + experience | Virtue (ἀρετή), eudaimonia | Dialectic, logic |
| **中世紀** | God as Being itself | Faith + reason | Divine law, natural law | Commentary, disputatio |
| **理性主義** | Substance dualism/monism | Innate ideas, a priori | Geometric deduction | Deduction, clear & distinct ideas |
| **經驗主義** | Bundle theory, idealism | Tabula rasa, a posteriori | Sentiment, utility | Induction, association |
| **德國唯心論** | Absolute spirit, transcendental | Synthetic a priori | Categorical imperative, Sittlichkeit | Dialectic, transcendental argument |
| **存在主義** | Existence over essence | Subjective truth, authenticity | Radical freedom, commitment | Phenomenology, indirect communication |
| **現象學** | Lifeworld, being-in-the-world | Intentionality, epoché | Lived experience | Descriptive, eidetic reduction |
| **分析哲學** | Logical atomism, Quinean desert | Linguistic analysis | Meta-ethics, non-cognitivism | Logical analysis, conceptual clarification |
| **實用主義** | Anti-representationalism | Fallibilism, inquiry | Meliorism, consequences | Pragmatic maxim, experimental |
| **後現代** | Anti-foundationalism | Perspectivism, deconstruction | Power critique, genealogy | Deconstruction, archaeology |

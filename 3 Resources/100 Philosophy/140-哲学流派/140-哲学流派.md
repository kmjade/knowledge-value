# 140 哲學流派 (Philosophical Schools)

> *"The history of philosophy is the history of heresy."* — Leszek Kołakowski
> 哲學史即異端史。

本 DDC 分類涵蓋西方哲學中的**主要流派與學派 (philosophical schools)**，從古希臘的先蘇哲學到二十世紀的後現代主義。每個流派代表一組共享的前提、方法論、核心問題與世界觀，它們互相辯證、滲透、推翻、復興，構成了哲學思維的歷史地層。

## 核心問題 Core Questions

1. **What is the ultimate nature of reality?** (Metaphysics)
   - Ancient: ἀρχή (Thales), Forms (Plato), substance (Aristotle)
   - Modern: monads (Leibniz), Absolute Spirit (Hegel), Being (Heidegger)

2. **How do we acquire knowledge?** (Epistemology)
   - Rationalism: innate ideas, a priori deduction
   - Empiricism: sensory experience, a posteriori induction
   - Kantian synthesis: transcendental conditions of experience

3. **What is the good life? How should we live?** (Ethics)
   - Hellenistic: ataraxia (Stoics/Epicureans/Skeptics)
   - Existentialism: authenticity, freedom, commitment
   - Pragmatism: practical consequences, meliorism

4. **What is the relationship between language, thought, and reality?** (Linguistic turn)
   - Analytic: logical analysis, meaning as use
   - Phenomenology: intentionality, lived experience
   - Postmodernism: deconstruction, discourse, power

## 學習路徑 Learning Path

推薦從古希臘開始，按照歷史順序閱讀，因為每個後續流派都是在回應、修正或反叛前一階段的問題：

```
古希臘 → 中世紀 → 理性主義/經驗主義 (平行閱讀)
    ↓
德國唯心論 (Kant 綜合理性與經驗)
    ↓
    ├→ 存在主義 (Kierkegaard, Nietzsche → Heidegger, Sartre)
    ├→ 現象學 (Husserl → Heidegger → Merleau-Ponty)
    ├→ 分析哲學 (Frege → Wittgenstein → Quine)
    └→ 實用主義/後現代 (Peirce/James → Rorty, Foucault, Derrida)
```

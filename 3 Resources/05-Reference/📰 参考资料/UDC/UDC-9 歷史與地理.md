# 9 歷史與地理 History and Geography

> 歷史與地理研究人類過去的活動和地球表面的空間分布，包括各國歷史、傳記、地理等。
> History and Geography study human past activities and spatial distribution on Earth's surface, including national histories, biographies, geography, etc.

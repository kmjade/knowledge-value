# 7 藝術與休閒 Arts and Recreation

> 藝術與休閒涵蓋人類創造性活動和娛樂活動，包括建築、繪畫、音樂、攝影、遊戲等。
> Arts and Recreation cover creative and recreational activities including architecture, painting, music, photography, games, etc.

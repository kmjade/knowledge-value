# WAN — Wide Area Network 廣域網路

> WAN connects geographically dispersed networks — cities, countries, continents.

## WAN Technologies

| Technology | Speed | Use Case |
|------------|-------|----------|
| **MPLS** | Up to 100 Gbps | Enterprise site-to-site |
| **SD-WAN** | Variable | Software-defined WAN |
| **Leased Line** | Fixed (E1/T1) | Dedicated point-to-point |
| **VPN over Internet** | Variable | Cost-effective site-to-site |
| **Satellite** | Up to 100 Mbps | Remote areas |

## MPLS vs SD-WAN

| Feature | MPLS | SD-WAN |
|---------|------|--------|
| Reliability | Very high | Depends on Internet |
| Cost | High | Lower |
| Flexibility | Low (carrier-managed) | High (software-defined) |
| QoS | Guaranteed | Best-effort (can improve) |

> 💡 Modern WAN is shifting from MPLS to SD-WAN — software-defined flexibility over hardware-defined reliability.

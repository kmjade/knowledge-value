# Wireless Networks 無線網路

## Wi-Fi Standards (IEEE 802.11)

| Standard | Year | Frequency | Max Speed |
|----------|------|-----------|-----------|
| 802.11b | 1999 | 2.4 GHz | 11 Mbps |
| 802.11g | 2003 | 2.4 GHz | 54 Mbps |
| 802.11n (Wi-Fi 4) | 2009 | 2.4/5 GHz | 600 Mbps |
| 802.11ac (Wi-Fi 5) | 2013 | 5 GHz | 6.9 Gbps |
| 802.11ax (Wi-Fi 6/6E) | 2019/2021 | 2.4/5/6 GHz | 9.6 Gbps |
| 802.11be (Wi-Fi 7) | 2024 | 2.4/5/6 GHz | 46 Gbps |

## CSMA/CA (vs CSMA/CD)
- Wireless can't detect collisions while transmitting
- **CA** = Collision Avoidance: RTS/CTS + random backoff

## Wi-Fi Security

| Protocol | Security Level | Status |
|----------|---------------|--------|
| WEP | Broken | ❌ Never use |
| WPA | Weak | ❌ Deprecated |
| WPA2 (AES) | Good | ✅ Current standard |
| WPA3 | Best | ✅ New standard |

> 💡 Wi-Fi 7 brings multi-link operation (MLO) — simultaneous use of multiple bands for higher throughput and lower latency.

# Learning Notes 學習心得

## Key Insights

1. **Layers are everything** — Once you internalize the OSI model, every problem becomes tractable. Ask "at which layer?"

2. **Encapsulation is the secret** — The same data gets wrapped and unwrapped at each layer. Understanding this explains everything from VPNs to VLANs.

3. **Trade-offs everywhere** — TCP vs UDP, MPLS vs SD-WAN, security vs performance. There's no perfect solution, only trade-offs.

4. **Wireshark is your best teacher** — Reading about TCP is one thing. Seeing the three-way handshake in Wireshark is another.

## Study Tips

- Build a home lab (GNS3, Packet Tracer)
- Capture and analyze your own traffic
- Read RFCs for protocols you use daily
- Explain concepts to others (Feynman technique)

> 💡 The best network engineers aren't those who memorized all the protocols — they're those who understand the principles and can debug systematically.

# 黄帝内经-灵枢-寒热第七十

## 📖 原文

黄帝问于岐伯曰：寒热瘰疬，在于颈腋者，何气使然？

岐伯曰：此皆鼠瘘寒热之毒气也。其本在于脏，其末在于颈腋。鼠瘘之本，在于足少阳胆经之脉，其末在于颈腋。其始也，起于足少阳，上循头角，下颌颊，入缺盆，下腋，循胸，过季胁，入缺盆，下膈，属肝络胆，上贯膈，布胁肋，循喉咙之后，上入颃颡，连目系，出额，上抵头角，下耳中，循颊车，下大迎，循颊车下颊，合于阳明之经。

黄帝曰：寒热之气，何因而生？

岐伯曰：寒热之气，生于阴阳之不和。阳气盛则身热，阴气盛则身寒。阳胜则热，阴胜则寒。阳胜则阴病，阴胜则阳病。阳胜则身热，腠理开，故身汗出；阴胜则身寒，腠理闭，故无汗。此寒热之理也。

黄帝曰：善。然则鼠瘘者，何气使然？

岐伯曰：鼠瘘者，寒热之毒气，留于经络之中，不得消散，发于颈腋，其形如鼠，故谓之鼠瘘。其始小，其渐大，其溃则如鼠穴，故名鼠瘘。其毒气根于脏腑，流于经络，发于颈腋，而成鼠瘘。

黄帝曰：善。然则鼠瘘可治乎？

岐伯曰：可治也。治之奈何？当以针治之。其法有五：一曰刺其本，二曰刺其末，三曰刺其经，四曰刺其络，五曰刺其穴。刺其本者，刺足少阳之原穴丘墟；刺其末者，刺颈腋之局部；刺其经者，刺足少阳之经脉；刺其络者，刺其络脉；刺其穴者，刺其阿是穴。五法并用，无不愈者。

黄帝曰：善。愿闻其治法。

岐伯曰：治鼠瘘者，凡三刺。一刺其肿，二刺其根，三刺其穴。肿者，刺其肿处，泄其毒气；根者，刺其根本，绝其病源；穴者，刺其要穴，调其气血。刺已，以药敷之，则愈矣。

## 🌐 白话文翻译

黄帝问岐伯说：寒热瘰疬发生在颈腋部位的，是什么气导致的呢？

岐伯说：这些都是鼠瘘寒热的毒气。它的根本在内脏，末梢在颈腋部位。鼠瘘的根本在于足少阳胆经的经脉，末梢在颈腋部位。开始时，起于足少阳，上行循行于头角，下行到下颌颊部，进入缺盆，下行到腋部，循行于胸部，经过季胁，进入缺盆，下行到膈肌，属于肝络胆，上行贯穿膈肌，散布于胁肋部，循行于喉咙之后，上行进入颃颡，连接目系，出于额部，上行到头角，下行进入耳中，循行于颊车，下行到大迎，循行于颊车下到颊部，会合于阳明的经脉。

黄帝说：寒热之气，是从什么原因产生的？

岐伯说：寒热之气，产生于阴阳的不和。阳气盛则身体发热，阴气盛则身体发冷。阳胜则热，阴胜则寒。阳胜则阴病，阴胜则阳病。阳胜则身热，腠理张开，所以身体出汗；阴胜则身寒，腠理闭合，所以无汗。这就是寒热的道理。

黄帝说：好。那么鼠瘘是什么气导致的呢？

岐伯说：鼠瘘是寒热的毒气，停留在经络中，不能消散，发于颈腋部位，其形状如老鼠，所以叫做鼠瘘。开始时很小，逐渐变大，溃烂时如鼠穴，所以名叫鼠瘘。它的毒气根植于脏腑，流行于经络，发于颈腋部位，形成鼠瘘。

黄帝说：好。那么鼠瘘可以治疗吗？

岐伯说：可以治疗。怎么治疗呢？应当用针刺治疗。方法有五种：一称刺其根本，二称刺其末梢，三称刺其经脉，四称刺其络脉，五称刺其穴位。刺其根本是刺足少阳的原穴丘墟；刺其末梢是刺颈腋的局部；刺其经脉是刺足少阳的经脉；刺其络脉是刺其络脉；刺其穴位是刺其阿是穴。五种方法并用，没有不痊愈的。

黄帝说：好。我想听听治疗方法。

岐伯说：治疗鼠瘘，有三种刺法。一刺其肿胀处，二刺其根部，三刺其穴位。刺肿胀处是刺肿胀部位，泄其毒气；刺根部是刺其根部，断绝其病源；刺穴位是刺其要穴，调其气血。刺完后，用药物敷贴，就会痊愈。

## 🌍 English Interpretation

The Yellow Emperor asked Qi Bo: When cold-heat and scrofula occur in the neck and axilla regions, what qi causes this?

Qi Bo replied: These are all toxic qi of rat fistula cold-heat. Its root is in the zang organs, and its branch is in the neck and axilla. The root of rat fistula lies in the Gallbladder meridian of Foot Shaoyang, and its branch is in the neck and axilla. Initially, it starts from Foot Shaoyang, ascends along the head angles, descends to the lower jaw and cheeks, enters the supraclavicular fossa, descends to the axilla, follows the chest, passes the hypochondriac region, enters the supraclavicular fossa, descends to the diaphragm, pertains to the Liver and connects with the Gallbladder, ascends to pierce the diaphragm, spreads over the hypochondrium, follows behind the pharynx, ascends to enter the nasopharynx, connects with the eye system, emerges at the forehead, ascends to the head angles, descends into the ears, follows the cheek bone, descends to Great Welcome (ST5), follows below the cheek bone to the cheeks, and joins with the Yangming meridian.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. Then from what cause does cold-heat qi arise?

Qi Bo replied: Cold-heat qi arises from disharmony of yin and yang. When yang qi is abundant, the body becomes hot; when yin qi is abundant, the body becomes cold. Yang predominance causes heat; yin predominance causes cold. When yang predominates, yin becomes diseased; when yin predominates, yang becomes diseased. When yang predominates, the body becomes hot and the couli opens, causing sweating; when yin predominates, the body becomes cold and the couli closes, causing absence of sweat. This is the principle of cold-heat.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. Then what causes rat fistula?

Qi Bo replied: Rat fistula is the toxic qi of cold-heat that remains in the meridians and cannot disperse. It manifests in the neck and axilla regions. Its shape is like a rat, hence it is called rat fistula. Initially, it is small, gradually grows larger, and when it ulcerates, it looks like a rat hole, thus the name rat fistula. Its toxic qi is rooted in the zang-fu organs, flows through the meridians, and manifests in the neck and axilla to form rat fistula.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. Then can rat fistula be treated?

Qi Bo replied: It can be treated. How should it be treated? It should be treated with acupuncture. There are five methods: first is needling the root, second is needling the branch, third is needling the meridian, fourth is needling the collaterals, fifth is needling the acupoints. Needling the root involves needling the source point Qiuxu (GB40) of the Foot Shaoyang; needling the branch involves needling the local area of the neck and axilla; needling the meridian involves needling the Foot Shaoyang meridian; needling the collaterals involves needling the collateral vessels; needling the acupoints involves needling the ashi points. Using these five methods together, none fail to recover.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. I would like to hear about the treatment methods.

Qi Bo replied: In treating rat fistula, there are three needling methods. First needling the swelling, second needling the root, third needling the acupoints. Needling the swelling means needling the swollen area to drain the toxic qi; needling the root means needling the root to cut off the disease source; needling the acupoints means needling the key points to regulate qi and blood. After needling, apply medicinal plasters and recovery will follow.

## 🔍 核心要点

### 寒热瘰疬病机
- **毒气来源**：鼠瘘寒热之毒气
- **发病机制**：本在脏、末在颈腋
- **经络所过**：足少阳胆经循行路径
- **病理演变**：始小→渐大→溃如鼠穴

### 寒热病机
- **阴阳不和**：寒热之气生于阴阳之不和
- **阳盛则热**：阳气盛→身热→腠理开→身汗出
- **阴盛则寒**：阴气盛→身寒→腠理闭→无汗
- **相互影响**：阳胜则阴病，阴胜则阳病

### 鼠瘘形成
- **毒气停滞**：寒热毒气，留于经络中
- **不得消散**：毒气久留，不能消散
- **发于颈腋**：毒气发于颈腋特定部位
- **形如老鼠**：形状如老鼠，故名鼠瘘

### 治疗五法
- **刺其本**：刺足少阳之原穴丘墟(GB40)
- **刺其末**：刺颈腋之局部
- **刺其经**：刺足少阳之经脉
- **刺其络**：刺其络脉
- **刺其穴**：刺其阿是穴

### 治疗三刺
- **刺其肿**：刺肿胀处→泄毒气
- **刺其根**：刺根部→绝病源
- **刺其穴**：刺要穴→调气血

## 📚 理论解释

### 经络循行理论
本章详细描述了足少阳胆经的循行路线，体现了经络学说的重要性。胆经从头到脚循行全身，其经气失调可导致颈腋部位的病变，这为中医的"循经取穴"理论提供了依据。

### 寒热病机理论
"生于阴阳之不和"是中医寒热证的核心病机。阳盛则热，阴盛则寒，不仅解释了发热恶寒的机理，还说明了腠理开合、汗出不汗等临床表现的理论基础。

### 毒邪致病理论
鼠瘘的形成体现了中医毒邪致病的理论。毒邪"根于脏腑，流于经络，发于颈腋"，说明毒邪既是外感六淫，也与内伤脏腑密切相关。"不得消散"强调邪气留聚是疾病发生发展的关键。

### 局部整体治疗观
本章治疗采用"刺本、刺末、刺经、刺络、刺穴"五法并用，既有整体调理（刺本、刺经），又有局部治疗（刺末、刺肿），体现了中医整体与局部相结合的治疗思想。

## 🏥 实践应用

### 诊断要点
1. **部位特点**：颈腋部位出现结节肿块
2. **症状表现**：初起如豆，渐大如鸡卵，溃烂如鼠穴
3. **寒热表现**：伴有寒热往来、汗出或无汗
4. **经络辨证**：足少阳胆经循行部位病变

### 针刺治疗
1. **整体取穴**：丘墟(GB40)为足少阳原穴，调理胆经
2. **局部取穴**：颈腋局部阿是穴、排刺
3. **循经取穴**：足少阳胆经穴位如风池(GB20)、肩井(GB21)
4. **配穴原则**：根据病情随证加减

### 药物治疗
1. **清热解毒**：金银花、连翘、蒲公英、野菊花
2. **软坚散结**：夏枯草、浙贝母、玄参、牡蛎
3. **活血化瘀**：丹参、赤芍、桃仁、红花
4. **扶正祛邪**：黄芪、党参、白术、茯苓

### 外治方法
1. **药物外敷**：消肿散结、解毒排脓类药物外敷
2. **切开排脓**：已成脓者切开引流
3. **药线引流**：脓腔较深者用药线引流
4. **术后护理**：定期换药，促进愈合

### 现代临床意义
1. **淋巴结核**：鼠瘘相当于现代医学的淋巴结结核
2. **淋巴瘤**：部分表现为颈部肿块者需鉴别
3. **转移癌**：颈部淋巴结转移癌的鉴别诊断
4. **免疫疾病**：免疫功能异常导致的淋巴结肿大

### 预防调摄
1. **生活调理**：作息规律，避免过劳
2. **饮食调养**：清淡饮食，忌辛辣刺激
3. **情志调摄**：保持心情舒畅，避免忧思郁怒
4. **增强体质**：适度锻炼，提高正气

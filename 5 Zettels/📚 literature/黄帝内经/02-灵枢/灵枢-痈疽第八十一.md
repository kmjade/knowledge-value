# 黄帝内经-灵枢-痈疽第八十一

## 📖 原文

黄帝曰：余闻古之治病者，惟视其虚实，补泻不失其宜，而疾已。今之医者，不然也，概以药攻之，不问其虚实，不知补泻，而病留。是以病益深，药益乱，而病不除，何也？

岐伯曰：此不知诊脉之道也。夫脉者，血之府也。长则气治，短则气病，数则烦心，大则病进，上盛则气高，下盛则气胀，代则气衰，细则气少，涩则心痛。浑浑革至如涌泉，病进而危；弊弊绰绰其去如弦绝者，死。此虚实之要，补泻之机也。

黄帝曰：善。然则痈疽者，何如？

岐伯曰：痈疽者，营卫之所留也，寒胜于热，则血凝而不行，不行则热胜于血，热胜血腐，则成痈疽。痈者，壅也；疽者，沮也。

黄帝曰：善。愿闻其形。

岐伯曰：痈者，皮肉之间，肿起色赤，根脚不大，按之热，痛者易已。疽者，发于筋骨之间，肿硬不赤，不热，不痛，根脚深大，皮色不变，难愈也。

黄帝曰：善。愿闻其处所。

岐伯曰：痈疽所发，随经络而行。发于阳经者，其形大而赤，其根浅而易治；发于阴经者，其形小而硬，其根深而难治。发于头面者，其形上而大；发于四肢者，其形下而小。发于背者，多为痈；发于腹者，多为疽。

黄帝曰：善。愿闻其刺法。

岐伯曰：痈疽之刺，凡三刺。一刺其上，二刺其下，三刺其旁。上刺者，刺其邪气；下刺者，刺其热气；旁刺者，刺其毒气。刺已，以热熨之，勿用冷水，冷水则血凝，血凝则邪留，邪留则病益甚。

黄帝曰：善。愿闻其药法。

岐伯曰：痈疽之药，凡三品。上品去腐生肌，中品清热解毒，下品消肿止痛。上药如生地、当归、川芎、白芷之类；中药如黄连、黄芩、黄柏、栀子之类；下药如大黄、芒硝、甘草之类。随证加减，不可拘泥。

## 🌐 白话文翻译

黄帝说：我听说古时候的医生，只看病情的虚实，补泻得当，疾病就好了。现在的医生不是这样，一味用药攻击疾病，不问虚实，不知补泻，导致疾病留着不去。因此疾病日益加深，用药日益混乱，而疾病不除，这是为什么呢？

岐伯说：这是不懂诊脉的道理。脉是血液聚集的地方。脉长则气调，脉短则气病，脉数则心烦，脉大则病进，上盛则气高，下盛则气胀，脉代则气衰，脉细则气少，脉涩则心痛。浑浊杂乱如涌泉而来，是疾病进展而危急；细弱紊乱如弦绝而去，是死证。这是虚实的要点，补泻的关键。

黄帝说：好。那么痈疽是怎么回事呢？

岐伯说：痈疽是营卫之气停留的地方，寒气胜过热气，则血液凝固而不运行，不运行则热气胜过血液，热气胜则血液腐败，就形成痈疽。痈，是壅塞的意思；疽，是腐蚀的意思。

黄帝说：好。我想听听它的形态。

岐伯说：痈，发生在皮肉之间，肿起颜色发红，根脚不大，按压发热，疼痛的容易痊愈。疽，发生在筋骨之间，肿硬不发红，不发热，不疼痛，根脚深且大，皮色不变，难以痊愈。

黄帝说：好。我想听听它们发生的部位。

岐伯说：痈疽的发病，沿着经络循行发生在阳经的，形态大而发红，根脚浅而容易治疗；发生在阴经的，形态小而坚硬，根脚深而难以治疗。发生在头面部的，形态在上方而较大；发生在四肢的，形态在下方而较小。发生在背部的，多为痈；发生在腹部的，多为疽。

黄帝说：好。我想听听针刺方法。

岐伯说：痈疽的针刺，共有三种刺法。第一刺上部，第二刺下部，第三刺旁边。上刺是刺其邪气；下刺是刺其热气；旁刺是刺其毒气。刺完后，用热熨的方法，不要用冷水，冷水会导致血液凝固，血液凝固则邪气留着，邪气留着则疾病更加严重。

黄帝说：好。我想听听用药方法。

岐伯说：痈疽的用药，共有三类。上品是去腐生肌的药物，中品是清热解毒的药物，下品是消肿止痛的药物。上药如生地、当归、川芎、白芷之类；中药如黄连、黄芩、黄柏、栀子之类；下药如大黄、芒硝、甘草之类。根据证候加减，不可拘泥。

## 🌍 English Interpretation

The Yellow Emperor said: I heard that in ancient times, physicians only observed the deficiency or excess of conditions, applied tonification or drainage appropriately, and the illness would resolve. Today's physicians are not like this - they just use drugs to attack the illness without asking about deficiency or excess, not knowing about tonification or drainage, leaving the illness lingering. Thus the illness deepens, the medication becomes more chaotic, and the illness is not eliminated. Why is this?

Qi Bo said: This is not understanding the way of pulse diagnosis. The pulse is the mansion of blood. A long pulse indicates regulated qi, a short pulse indicates qi illness, a rapid pulse indicates vexation of the heart, a large pulse indicates advancing illness, upper excess indicates high qi, lower excess indicates qi distension, an intermittent pulse indicates declining qi, a fine pulse indicates scant qi, and a rough pulse indicates heart pain. Mud-like and chaotic arriving like a gushing spring indicates advancing illness and danger; faint and chaotic departing like a broken string indicates death. This is the essential of deficiency and excess, the mechanism of tonification and drainage.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. So what about carbuncles and abscesses?

Qi Bo said: Carbuncles and abscesses are where the ying and wei qi accumulate. When cold overcomes heat, blood congeals and doesn't flow. When it doesn't flow, heat overcomes blood. When heat overcomes blood, it putrefies, forming carbuncles and abscesses. "Yong" (痈) means obstruction; "ju" (疽) means putrefaction.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. I would like to hear about their forms.

Qi Bo said: Carbuncles occur between the skin and flesh, swelling up with red color, with not-large roots. When pressed they are hot and painful - these are easy to resolve. Abscesses occur between the sinews and bones, are hard and not red, not hot, not painful, with deep and large roots and unchanged skin color - these are difficult to resolve.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. I would like to hear about their locations.

Qi Bo said: The occurrence of carbuncles and abscesses follows the meridians. Those occurring on yang meridians have large forms that are red, with shallow roots that are easy to treat; those occurring on yin meridians have small, hard forms with deep roots that are difficult to treat. Those occurring on the head and face are above and large; those occurring on the limbs are below and small. Those occurring on the back are mostly carbuncles; those occurring on the abdomen are mostly abscesses.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. I would like to hear about the needling methods.

Qi Bo said: The needling of carbuncles and abscesses has three methods. First needle the upper part, second needle the lower part, third needle the side. Upper needling needles the evil qi; lower needling needles the heat qi; side needling needles the toxic qi. After needling, apply hot fomentation, not cold water. Cold water causes blood congealment; congealed blood causes lingering evil qi; lingering evil qi causes the illness to worsen.

The Yellow Emperor said: Good. I would like to hear about the medicinal methods.

Qi Bo said: The medicinal treatment of carbuncles and abscesses has three categories. The upper grade removes putrefaction and generates muscle; the middle grade clears heat and resolves toxins; the lower grade reduces swelling and alleviates pain. Upper grade medicines include things like sheng di (rehmannia), dang gui (angelica), chuan xiong (ligusticum), and bai zhi (angelica dahurica); middle grade medicines include things like huang lian (coptis), huang qin (scutellaria), huang bai (phellodendron), and zhi zi (gardenia); lower grade medicines include things like da huang (rhubarb), mang xiao (mirabilite), and gan cao (licorice). Add or subtract according to the pattern; do not be rigid.

## 🔍 核心要点

### 诊脉之道
- **脉象与气血**：脉为血之府，反映气血状态
- **脉象要素**：长、短、数、大、上盛、下盛、代、细、涩
- **预后判断**：浑浑革至如涌泉（病进而危）、弊弊绰绰其去如弦绝（死证）
- **虚实要机**：虚实之要，补泻之机

### 痈疽病机
- **营卫停留**：营卫之所留也
- **寒热交争**：寒胜于热→血凝不行→热胜血腐
- **痈疽形成**：痈者壅也（壅塞）、疽者沮也（腐蚀）

### 痈疽形态鉴别
- **痈的特点**：皮肉之间、肿起色赤、根脚不大、按之热痛、易已
- **疽的特点**：筋骨之间、肿硬不赤、不热不痛、根脚深大、难愈

### 发病部位规律
- **经络分型**：阳经（形大而赤、根浅易治）、阴经（形小而硬、根深难治）
- **部位特点**：头面（上而大）、四肢（下而小）、背部（多为痈）、腹部（多为疽）

### 治疗方法
- **针刺三法**：上刺邪气、下刺热气、旁刺毒气
- **热熨忌水**：刺已热熨，勿用冷水
- **药品三品**：上品去腐生肌、中品清热解毒、下品消肿止痛

## 📚 理论解释

### 外科理论基础
本章是《灵枢》中最为完整的外科篇章，系统阐述了痈疽的病因病机、诊断鉴别、治疗原则，是中医外科理论的经典文献。痈疽理论体现了中医外科"辨证论治"的核心思想。

### 气血津液理论
痈疽的病机核心在于气血津液运行的障碍。"营卫所留"导致气机不畅，"血凝不行"导致血瘀，"热胜血腐"导致组织坏死，这种从气到血、从功能到形态的变化规律，是中医病理学的重要认识。

### 经络辨证思想
痈疽发病与经络循行密切相关，阳经发病与阴经发病在症状、治疗预后上有明显差异，体现了经络辨证在中医外科的重要地位。不同部位的痈疽特点各异，反映了"同病异症"、"异病同症"的中医辨证思维。

### 整体治疗观
痈疽治疗采用了针刺、外治、内治等多种方法，体现了中医整体治疗观。上刺邪气、下刺热气、旁刺毒气的针刺原则，以及药品三品的分类应用，都体现了中医治疗的有序性和层次性。

## 🏥 实践应用

### 诊断鉴别
1. **痈疽鉴别**：根据部位、形态、颜色、温度、疼痛程度等鉴别诊断
2. **预后判断**：痈易治而疽难治，阳经易治而阴经难治
3. **经络辨证**：根据痈疽部位判断所属经络，指导经络论治

### 针刺治疗
1. **三刺法的应用**：上刺邪气、下刺热气、旁刺毒气
2. **刺后护理**：热熨为主，禁止冷水浸泡
3. **穴位选择**：根据痈疽局部及所属经络选穴

### 药物治疗
1. **上品药应用**：生地、当归、川芎、白芷（活血生肌）
2. **中品药应用**：黄连、黄芩、黄柏、栀子（清热解毒）
3. **下品药应用**：大黄、芒硝、甘草（消肿止痛）
4. **随证加减**：根据具体症状灵活配伍

### 外科预防
1. **避免外伤**：保护皮肤，避免破损感染
2. **调理气血**：保持气血流通，预防营卫留滞
3. **生活调理**：饮食有节，起居有常，增强正气

### 现代临床意义
1. **感染性疾病**：痈疽理论对现代感染性疾病的诊治有指导意义
2. **伤口愈合**：去腐生肌理论对现代伤口愈合研究有启发
3. **肿瘤防治**：痈疽的早期发现和治疗原则对肿瘤防治有参考价值

# 6 應用科學 Applied Sciences

> 應用科學將自然科學的原理應用於實際問題，包括醫學、工程、農業、商業等領域。
> Applied Sciences apply principles of natural sciences to practical problems, including medicine, engineering, agriculture, business, etc.

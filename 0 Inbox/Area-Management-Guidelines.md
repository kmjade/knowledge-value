### What is an Area?
An Area is a broad category or domain that represents a significant aspect of your life or work. It serves as a container for related Projects and Resources.

### Characteristics of a Good Area:
- **Broad but focused**: Covers a meaningful life domain
- **Strategic importance**: Aligned with your long-term goals
- **Active management**: Requires regular attention and review
- **Resource-rich**: Contains multiple Projects and Resources

### Examples of Areas:
- Health & Wellness
- Career Development
- Finance & Wealth Building
- Relationships & Family
- Learning & Education
- Lifestyle & Hobbies

### Step 1: Identification
1. **Brainstorm**: List all significant life domains
2. **Prioritize**: Rank by importance and relevance

### Step 2: Template Application
Use the standardized Area template with:

- Review schedule

### Step 3: Initial Setup
- Create the Area note

## ğŸ¯ Best Practices

### Do's
1. **Keep Areas focused**: Each Area should have a clear purpose
2. **Regular reviews**: Maintain consistent review schedules
3. **Link strategically**: Connect related content effectively
4. **Archive promptly**: Move completed content to Archives
5. **Update metrics**: Keep progress tracking current
6. **Review templates**: Ensure templates remain useful

### Don'ts
1. **Don't create too many Areas**: 6-12 Areas is ideal
2. **Don't neglect reviews**: Outdated Areas become useless
3. **Don't duplicate content**: Use linking instead
4. **Don't make Areas too specific**: Keep them broad enough to be meaningful
5. **Don't abandon Areas**: Either maintain them or archive them
6. **Don't skip the template**: Consistency is key

# First-CN-TW (繁體中文優先)

Review and ensure all content follows Traditional Chinese-first principles:

1. **Code**: Comments, commit messages, and documentation in Traditional Chinese
2. **Notes**: Note titles, tags, and content in Traditional Chinese
3. **Characters**: Convert Simplified Chinese to Traditional Chinese where appropriate
4. **Configuration**: Frontmatter and settings can use Traditional Chinese

Check the current file/selection and apply Traditional Chinese-first conventions where appropriate.
